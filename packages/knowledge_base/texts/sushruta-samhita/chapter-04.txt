Now we shall discuss the Chapter which deals with General Explanations (Prabha'Saniya- madhyaryam). The endeavours of a man who has studied the entire Ayurveda (shastra) but fails to make a clear exposition of the same, are vain like the efforts of an ass that carries a load of sandal wood (without ever being able to enjoy its pleasing scent).
